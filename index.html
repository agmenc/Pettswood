Pettswood by agmenc

Specification testing for Scala

Whether you call it acceptance testing or specification testing, Pettswood lets you create tests for you system that also act as living documentation, or even a user manual. The tests are expressed as HTML, so you have a great deal of flexibility with how they look.

Depdendencies

Scala, sbt and a JVM
